Biryani

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat, or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes. It's fire.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg Chicken
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 bowls of Rice
  • 3 tablespoons yoghurt
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Put chicken in pan. Fry it.
  2. Put in rice.
  3. Add masala and salt.
  4. Enjoy!
